Who we are

Kangocho Alumni Association is a body of students who have completed their studies in secondary school education through Kangocho Secondary School and are bound together by a common interest in Education and the Nation building, and IT declares that it shall be non-political, non-denominational, voluntal and non-partisan AND FURTHER DECLARES that no provision therein shall or will be construed as to qualify defeat or in any manner contravene the constitution of the republic of Kenya or Holy Scriptures and such provision shall to the extent of contravention have no effect WITHOUT PREJUDICE to the foregoing it is specifically provided as hereunder;

Our Purpose

  1. Conduct mentorship programs to students at Kangocho Secondary School and to members of the Alumni
  2. Liaise with Kangocho Secondary School, and where necessary ,work with the school to promote education and wholesome development to the students therein
  3. Shall promote any ethical, legal and lawful agricultural and entrepreneurial activities among its members
